Products

Renowned for its exquisite jewelry, Tiffany offers a diverse range of fine pieces, from the iconic Tiffany Setting engagement rings to masterfully crafted diamond jewelry and colorful gemstone creations. Beyond jewelry, their offerings extend to sterling silver accessories, crystal homeware, porcelain, watches, fragrances, stationery, haute couture fragrance, personal accessories, and leather goods. Each product embodies the brand's dedication to timeless design, exceptional craftsmanship, and the use of premium materials.

07


Packaging

Tiffany & Co.'s packaging is as iconic as its jewelry, recognized worldwide for its distinctive elegance and the coveted "Tiffany Blue" color. The packaging, often referred to as the "Tiffany Blue Box," features a soft, robin's egg blue hue, which is instantly recognizable and symbolizes luxury. These boxes are often tied with a white satin ribbon, further enhancing the sense of sophistication and exclusivity. The company's attention to detail extends to the packaging, where each item is carefully placed and presented to create a sense of anticipation and excitement, reinforcing the notion that opening a Tiffany blue box is as much a part of the luxury experience as the exquisite jewelry contained within.

Popular Items

Tiffany & Co. boasts a portfolio of iconic and popular items that have become synonymous with luxury and timeless elegance. Among these, the Tiffany Setting engagement ring stands out as a symbol of enduring love and sophistication. The Return to Tiffany collection, featuring the classic heart tag pendant and bracelet, has achieved cult status and is beloved for its simple yet elegant design. The Tiffany T collection embodies modern style, while the Atlas collection showcases the brand's commitment to clean lines and precision.
